May, 1976 - The Start Of The Lower Mainland Yacht Co-Op

Written in 2006 on the Club's 30th Anniversary

In 1980 there were 30 members, still one boat. By 1984 there were 100 members and four boats. Recently, the group has grown to 115 sailing members.

Through the years the fleet has grown to seven sailboats: three 26' Grampians, two 27' C; an Ericson 28+ and a 1983 Cal 25 MKII.

Dreaming of sailing up to the Gulf Islands without the hassle of owning your own boat?
